Bellows

Flexible Solutions for Piping Systems

Precision-Manufactured Bellows

Alamdar International manufactures high-quality bellows designed to handle thermal expansion, vibration, and misalignment in piping systems and industrial equipment. Our bellows are precision-engineered to provide reliable performance in demanding applications.

Available in various materials including stainless steel, carbon steel, and exotic alloys, our bellows are manufactured using advanced techniques to ensure consistent quality and performance. They are designed to accommodate axial, lateral, and angular movements while maintaining system integrity.

Types of Bellows

Axial Bellows

Designed to accommodate axial movement (compression and extension) in piping systems. Ideal for applications with thermal expansion in straight pipe runs.

  • Axial compression and extension
  • High pressure capability
  • Compact design

Lateral Bellows

Engineered to handle lateral (perpendicular) movement and offset in piping systems. Suitable for applications with misalignment or lateral displacement.

  • Lateral movement accommodation
  • Offset compensation
  • Flexible installation

Universal Bellows

Versatile bellows capable of handling axial, lateral, and angular movements simultaneously. Perfect for complex piping configurations.

  • Multi-directional movement
  • Angular rotation capability
  • Complex system applications

Tied Bellows

Bellows with tie rods to prevent over-extension and control movement. Used in applications requiring controlled expansion limits.

  • Controlled movement
  • Over-extension protection
  • Precise positioning

Applications

Power Generation

Bellows for steam lines, turbine connections, and high-temperature piping systems in power plants

Petrochemical

Expansion joints for refineries, chemical plants, and petrochemical processing facilities

HVAC Systems

Flexible connections for heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems

Exhaust Systems

Bellows for engine exhaust, boiler flue gas, and industrial exhaust applications

Pump Connections

Vibration isolation and movement accommodation for pump piping connections

Turbine Systems

Expansion compensation for gas and steam turbine piping connections
