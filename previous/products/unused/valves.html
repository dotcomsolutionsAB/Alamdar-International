Valves

Precision Flow Control Solutions

Comprehensive Valve Solutions

Alamdar International offers a comprehensive range of high-quality valves designed for various industrial applications. Our valves are precision-engineered to provide optimal flow control, leak-proof reliability, and long-lasting durability.

We manufacture and supply valves that meet API standards and adhere to global industry specifications. Our product range includes gate valves, globe valves, ball valves, butterfly valves, and check valves, each designed to meet specific operational requirements.

Our Valve Range

Gate Valves

Gate valves are designed for on/off service with minimal pressure drop. They feature a wedge-shaped gate that moves perpendicular to the flow direction, providing tight shutoff and excellent flow characteristics.

  • Full bore design for minimal pressure drop
  • Suitable for high-pressure applications
  • Available in various materials and sizes
  • Compliant with API standards

Globe Valves

Globe valves provide excellent throttling capabilities and precise flow control. The disc moves perpendicular to the seat, allowing for fine regulation of flow rates.

  • Excellent throttling capabilities
  • Precise flow control
  • Good shutoff characteristics
  • Wide range of applications

Ball Valves

Ball valves offer quick on/off operation with a quarter-turn mechanism. The spherical closure element provides excellent sealing and minimal maintenance requirements.

  • Quick quarter-turn operation
  • Excellent sealing properties
  • Low maintenance requirements
  • Versatile applications

Butterfly Valves

Butterfly valves feature a disc that rotates to control flow. They are compact, lightweight, and provide excellent flow control for large diameter applications.

  • Compact and lightweight design
  • Cost-effective solution
  • Suitable for large diameters
  • Easy operation and maintenance

Check Valves

Check valves prevent backflow in piping systems. They automatically open when flow is in the forward direction and close when flow reverses.

  • Automatic backflow prevention
  • No external actuation required
  • Various designs available
  • Reliable operation
