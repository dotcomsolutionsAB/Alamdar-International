Pressure & Temperature Instruments

Precision Measurement & Control Solutions

Accurate Measurement Solutions

Alamdar International offers a comprehensive range of pressure and temperature instruments designed for accurate measurement and control in various industrial processes. Our instruments are engineered for precision, reliability, and durability in demanding environments.

From basic gauges to advanced transmitters and controllers, we provide solutions that meet the diverse needs of industries including power generation, oil & gas, chemical processing, water treatment, and manufacturing.

Product Range

Pressure Gauges

High-quality pressure gauges for accurate pressure measurement in various applications. Available in different pressure ranges, sizes, and connection types.

  • Bourdon tube pressure gauges
  • Diaphragm pressure gauges
  • Digital pressure gauges
  • Differential pressure gauges
  • Various pressure ranges and accuracies

Temperature Gauges

Reliable temperature measurement instruments for monitoring process temperatures. Suitable for a wide range of temperature applications.

  • Bimetal temperature gauges
  • Liquid-filled temperature gauges
  • Digital temperature indicators
  • Thermocouple and RTD systems
  • Wide temperature range coverage

Pressure Transmitters

Advanced pressure transmitters for continuous monitoring and control. Provide accurate, reliable pressure measurement with electrical output signals.

  • 4-20mA output signals
  • HART communication protocol
  • High accuracy and stability
  • Various pressure ranges
  • Process and gauge pressure types

Temperature Transmitters

Precision temperature transmitters for accurate temperature measurement and transmission. Compatible with thermocouples and RTDs.

  • Thermocouple input types
  • RTD input support
  • Linearized output signals
  • Field-configurable options
  • High accuracy performance

Controllers

Process controllers for maintaining desired pressure and temperature setpoints. Available in various control modes and configurations.

  • PID control algorithms
  • On/off control modes
  • Digital display and setpoint adjustment
  • Alarm and output functions
  • Compact and panel-mount designs

Accessories

Complete range of accessories including pressure switches, temperature switches, gauge isolators, and mounting hardware.

  • Pressure and temperature switches
  • Gauge isolators and snubbers
  • Mounting brackets and hardware
  • Extension capillaries
  • Protective cases and covers

Applications

Power Generation

Pressure and temperature monitoring in boilers, turbines, and steam systems

Measurement and control in upstream, midstream, and downstream operations

Chemical Processing

Process monitoring and control in chemical and petrochemical plants

Water Treatment

Monitoring pressure and temperature in water and wastewater treatment facilities

Temperature and pressure control in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning

Manufacturing

Process monitoring and control in various manufacturing applications
