Cast Basalt

Superior Abrasion Resistance Solutions

Wear-Resistant Cast Basalt

Alamdar International manufactures high-quality cast basalt products that offer superior abrasion resistance and long-lasting performance in demanding material handling applications. Cast basalt is an ideal solution for protecting equipment from wear caused by abrasive materials.

Our cast basalt products are manufactured using natural basalt rock, which is melted and cast into various shapes and sizes. The resulting material exhibits exceptional hardness and wear resistance, making it perfect for applications involving abrasive materials.

Our Cast Basalt Product Range

Cast Basalt Pipes

Cast basalt pipes and tubes for handling abrasive materials in material transport systems. Available in various diameters and lengths.

  • Superior abrasion resistance
  • Various diameters and lengths
  • Easy installation
  • Long service life

Cast basalt linings for pipes, elbows, bends, and other components to protect against wear in material handling systems.
